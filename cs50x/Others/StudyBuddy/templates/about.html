About

AcademixBuddies is a platform for students in Singapore to search for study buddies. We hope to provide a support network for students not only academically but also emotionally. AcademixBuddies aims to match students of similar educational background so there is a mutual support system. We also hope to alleviate the stress that students go through by reminding them that they are not alone on this journey.
